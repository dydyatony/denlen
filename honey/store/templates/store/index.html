What is raw honey?

Raw honey is 100 % pure, unfiltered and unpasteurized sweetener created by bees from the nectar of flowers. Most honey eaten today is processed honey that’s been heated and filtered since it was collected from the hive. Unlike processed honey, raw honey does not get robbed of its vitamins and minerals. It also provides many health benefits!
